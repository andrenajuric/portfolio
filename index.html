About
Me

I am 24 years old. I graduated from UW Seattle in 2018 with a bachelor's degree in Sociology. I went in to UW as a pre-biology major. After a couple quarters, I realized it wasn't for me and I set my sights on computer science as it was always an interest of mine. After I struggled through the introductory class and received a barely passing grade, I was dissuaded away from continuing that pursuit. It has always been my biggest regret. Now I am enrolled in a coding bootcamp offered through UW, so that I can give it another try and hope that it leads to a career change.

As of right now, I am a customer service representative for Cadman Inc., a company that provides ready-mix concrete, asphalt, sand & gravel to contractors and homeowners. I have learned a lot within the past 2 years that I've worked there, but I lack passion in the work that I do. The work that I've done in this bootcamp so far has been extremely challenging, but also very rewarding. We've tackled basic HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, third-party API's, and we are now getting into Node. I recently completed my first group project; my group members and I developed a website using a third-party API that would recommend certain cocktails based on the users current location and time of day. I have learned a lot since the course has started, I practice every day, and I look forward to sharpening my skills so that I may someday translate these skills into an actual career.
